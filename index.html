States & Division

Myanmar is divided into a total of fourteen main regions, made up of seven states and seven divisions. These areas help organise the country based on geography, culture, and local identity, with each one offering its own unique landscapes, traditions, and ways of life. Together, they form the full picture of Myanmar’s diversity, from mountains and coastlines to busy cities and quiet rural areas.
